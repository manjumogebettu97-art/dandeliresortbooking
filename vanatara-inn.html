About the Stay

Your home away from home

Vanatara Inn sits in Pradhani, Dandeli — built around a large swimming pool with cottages and rooms that open onto private sit-outs and poolside views. It's made for families, friends and couples who want nature without giving up comfort.

Days here mix adventure and rest: river rafting, kayaking and boating on the Kali, zorbing and a rain dance to cool off, then a campfire with music as the evening settles in. An on-site food court and dining keep everyone fed between activities.

All activities depend on weather conditions and are subject to availability.
