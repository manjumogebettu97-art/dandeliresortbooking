About the Stay

A calmer way to plan Dandeli

Nadi Teera Cottages sits right on the banks of the river in Badgund, Ganeshgudi — clear pricing, meal timings and activity support all in one place, so you get a nature stay without last-minute confusion.

Choose from premium and deluxe air-conditioned cottages or budget commando cottages, all wrapped in forest and riverside views. Buffet meals, a swimming pool, riverside seating and bonfire nights round out the stay.

All activities depend on weather conditions and are subject to availability.
