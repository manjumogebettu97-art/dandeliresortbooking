About the Stay

Luxury, wrapped in jungle

Eco Green Dandeli is a jungle stay near KC Circle, built for travellers who want the forest close by without giving up the comforts — an infinity pool that looks out over the treeline, air-conditioned rooms and tidy housekeeping throughout.

Pick from deluxe sharing rooms, camping tents or a group dormitory, then fill the day with indoor games, archery and a bonfire at night. Veg and non-veg dining, WiFi, parking and laundry keep the whole trip easy.

All activities depend on weather conditions and are subject to availability.
